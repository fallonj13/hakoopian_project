Poway Project

A 19,000 sq foot estate tucked in the hills of Poway, CA. We joined forces with our client to bring some new life to this beautiful foundation. We took what were dark and heavy interiors to light and bright. We mixed warm whites with warm browns to give this home an update while keeping it true to its architectural bones. Organic wood textures compliment the home along with river stone giving dimension to the overall feel of the home.
