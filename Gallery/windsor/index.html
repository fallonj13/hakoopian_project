Windsor Project

A historical home located in the famous Hancock Park district of Los Angeles CA. We joined up with some of our favorite clients to restore this pillar of a home and bring it into todays era. This homes design direction gave a very transitional and yet modern touches throughout. This home came with all the build challenges you would expect from a 100 year old restoration and it was a daily grind to bring it to completion. A beautiful interior foundation made it a breeze to procure its furnishings and materials.
